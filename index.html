Welcome To The Study Helper!

As the name implies this website is one that is meant to help people who struggle to study do so.
You can pick the amount of time you want to study for, and it will keep track of it. It has features such as notes and to-do lists to help you stay on track and remember different things. You can customize it however you'd like by changing the background, or if it helps you study playing some music.
